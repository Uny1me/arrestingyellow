Logo Design.

For businesses just starting out, a simple logo design is enough for a visual representation of your brand.

To begin I do a simple research on your industry and potential competitors in the market. I then develop three (3) logo concepts of which you choose one. In two reviews we develop this logo further into different variations, after which you choose a final logo design that resonates with your brand.

- Questionnaire
- Moodboard
- 3 Logo Concepts, with variations
- Primary color palette
- Source files

TIMELINE : 21 DAYS

Brand Identity Design.

For existing businesses who want to go further with their visual representation, a full brand identity design is ideal.

The process begins with you providing me 4-5 keywords that describe your brand, as well as othe necessary information. I then do a research on the industry, your potential competitors ad your target audience. With this information we create 2 stylescape options, which we develop into 1 functional identity for your brand.

This package includes 2 stylescape options and 3 logo concepts from the preferred stylescape, with 2 reviews for all design elements. You can choose to add any more materials and deliverables*
After we have created a functional visual identity, I further provide a style guide and any other material to guide you in the proper use of your new brand identity.

*Prices change based upon amount and type of deliverables.

- Research
- Stylescape and Direction
- Logo concepts and variations
- Color system
- Font system
- Deliverables*
- Brand Guide
- Source Files

TIMELINE: 6-8 WEEKS

User Interface Design.

This package includes 2 layout concepts and 2 reviews of all elements.
