"I bu Nwoke?"

The Misconception Of Masculinity

In a society where light is shed on reactions and consequences, 'I bu Nwoke?' is an attempt to shift focus to actions and provocations. Everyone has something to say about violence against women, emotional disfunctionality in boys, power dynamics, female entitlement. Let us start saying something about our perception of masculinity, what it is, and what it ought not to be.
