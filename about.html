About Me.

Born in Lagos Nigeria in year 2000, Arresting Yellow is a visual artist, graphic and motion designer whose art aims at bringing to light the typically ignored intricacies of the human condition, such as street harassment and the misconceptions of boyhood in Africa through digital collage surrealism. As an advocate of mental health awareness and gender equality, Arresting Yellow reveals otherwise known but hidden repercussions of stereotypes and illusions in her vibrant collage style art. She is currently experimenting a fusion of collage surrealism, aesthetics and motion design.

I choose surrealism to depict intricacies that society now considers normal, because we cannot cause a shift in our thought process without having the things we consider norms depicted in a surreal manner. As a messenger, art is my microphone and I strive to contribute to the disruption of stereotypes imposed by society through collage surrealism.
